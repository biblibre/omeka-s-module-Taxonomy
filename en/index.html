Taxonomy allows to create controlled vocabularies within Omeka S.

Controlled vocabularies (taxonomies) and controlled vocabulary terms (taxonomy terms) are Omeka S resources, which means:

  • they can be linked to items, item sets and media like any other Omeka S resources (they can be linked in annotations too);

  • they can be described like any other resources (resource template, class, properties, annotations);

  • they can have a thumbnail.
